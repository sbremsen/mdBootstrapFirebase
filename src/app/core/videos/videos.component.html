Do Fries Go With that Shake?

Peakfest! -- Scotty R. Extraterrestrial Guitar

This is a live show from UCSD in La Jolla, San Diego, in the fall of 2012. Featuring Jerry Pavelko on drums/synthesizer and vocals during parts of "Her Disaster". This improvisation effectively creates a Sound Sculpture, as the music tightly weaves through interludes and harmonic phrases to take the listener on a wide-open journey.

by Scott Remsen/Jerry Pavelko, 09/12/2011
