Chi Club Studio Sessions

Songs from the original band demo, featuring Chi Club's original line-up.

The Chi Club is a band that started in Ocean Beach, San Diego, in 2005. The original band members recorded a five song demo album, featuring the instrumentals "Iced Chi" and "Swell", as well as "Pacific Breezes". Songs on this page are from the Chi EP.
